The Hook - What is Cavebreakers?

[Placeholder: Striking concept art - dark cave entrance lit by torchlight]

Elevator Pitch: A co-op survival horror game where 1-4 Stone Age scavengers must outwit terrifying mythical creatures and each other to escape a cursed wilderness.

Core Concept: Balance desperate teamwork with treacherous sabotage in a tense, atmospheric prehistoric world.

Genre: Multiplayer Co-op Survival Horror with Social Deduction Elements.

Quick Comps: Dead by Daylight meets The Forest with the tension of Left 4 Dead & betrayal of Among Us.

The Core Gameplay Loop

[Placeholder: Stylized flowchart graphic - cave painting style]
  • SEARCH: Spawn into a procedurally generated wilderness.
  • SCAVENGE: Gather essential resources (flint, wood, herbs, bones).
  • CRAFT: Create crude tools, torches, weapons, and cunning traps.
  • SECURE: Light vital Bonfires for safety, perks, and healing.
  • SURVIVE: Evade or confront deadly myths using stealth, wits & environment.
  • ESCAPE: Complete objectives before the darkness consumes all.
  • UPGRADE: Spend Artifacts on permanent perks for future hunts.

Key Pillars: Exploration, Resource Management, Strategic Evasion/Combat, Teamwork vs. Sabotage, Meta-Progression.

Encounter: The Werebear

While scavenging, you hear heavy footsteps. A massive Werebear bursts into view, sniffing aggressively. It hasn't spotted you yet. You see a dense thicket nearby and a cliff edge further away.

Outcome: Stealth!

You dive into the thicket. The Werebear snorts, glances around, but its poor eyesight fails it. It lumbers off.

Survival favored the cautious... this time.

Outcome: Risky Escape!

You sprint towards the cliff. The Werebear roars and gives chase! You leap across a narrow chasm just as the beast slams into the rockface.

Daring, but you might be separated now.

Outcome: Attention Gained!

Your rock clatters nearby. The Werebear snaps its head towards the sound... then locks eyes with YOU. It roars and charges!

Prepare to run... or fight!

The Twist: Sabotage Scenario

Headline: Trust is a Resource. Spend it Wisely.

You and a wounded teammate flee Werewolves. You reach a bonfire site, but only have flint to light it quickly for one before the pack arrives...

Outcome: Self-Preservation!

You quickly strike the flint, the bonfire roars, repelling the wolves as you stumble into its light. Your teammate's cry fades behind you.

You survived. Alone. You keep all the artifacts, but face 'Bad Karma'.

Outcome: Shared Risk!

You try to help your teammate while fumbling with the flint. The fire catches weakly as the wolves pounce! Maybe you fight them off? Maybe one distracts?

A gamble. Success means 'Survivor Bonus'. Failure means death...

(Sabotage creates intense, memorable moments!)
