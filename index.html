The Hook - What is Cavebreakers?

[Placeholder: Striking concept art - dark cave entrance lit by torchlight]

Elevator Pitch: A co-op survival horror game where 1-4 Stone Age scavengers must outwit terrifying mythical creatures and each other to escape a cursed wilderness.

Core Concept: Balance desperate teamwork with treacherous sabotage in a tense, atmospheric prehistoric world.

Genre: Multiplayer Co-op Survival Horror with Social Deduction Elements.

Quick Comps: Dead by Daylight meets The Forest with the tension of Left 4 Dead & betrayal of Among Us.

Meet the Horrors

Click a creature to learn a tactical tip.

[Image: Wendigo]

Wendigo

Ethereal mimic, fears fire, fast & stealthy. Exploit its fear & gaze aversion.

Tip: Its mimicry can lure you into traps. Use fire to force retreats. Aim for the head!

[Image: Werebear]

Werebear

Hulking brute, charges, tracks by scent. Lure into large traps or exploit its rage cooldown.

Tip: Listen for its heavy steps. Use large spike pits. Kite it around obstacles during its rage cooldown.

[Image: Werewolf]

Werewolf

Agile pack hunter, targets the isolated. Use coordination, loud noises & multiple lights.

Tip: Stick together! Use noise makers or combined torches to break their pack coordination.

Encounter: The Werebear

While scavenging, you hear heavy footsteps. A massive Werebear bursts into view, sniffing aggressively. It hasn't spotted you yet. You see a dense thicket nearby and a cliff edge further away.

Outcome: Stealth!

You dive into the thicket. The Werebear snorts, glances around, but its poor eyesight fails it. It lumbers off.

Survival favored the cautious... this time.

Outcome: Risky Escape!

You sprint towards the cliff. The Werebear roars and gives chase! You leap across a narrow chasm just as the beast slams into the rockface.

Daring, but you might be separated now.

Outcome: Attention Gained!

Your rock clatters nearby. The Werebear snaps its head towards the sound... then locks eyes with YOU. It roars and charges!

Prepare to run... or fight!

The Twist: Sabotage Scenario

Headline: Trust is a Resource. Spend it Wisely.

You and a wounded teammate flee Werewolves. You reach a bonfire site, but only have flint to light it quickly for one before the pack arrives...

Outcome: Self-Preservation!

You quickly strike the flint, the bonfire roars, repelling the wolves as you stumble into its light. Your teammate's cry fades behind you.

You survived. Alone. You keep all the artifacts, but face 'Bad Karma'.

Outcome: Shared Risk!

You try to help your teammate while fumbling with the flint. The fire catches weakly as the wolves pounce! Maybe you fight them off? Maybe one distracts?

A gamble. Success means 'Survivor Bonus'. Failure means death...

Art Style & Immersion

[Placeholder: Atmospheric collage - FP view hiding, bonfire, cave, UI mockup]
Atmosphere: Focus on dense foliage, volumetric fog, detailed textures, and environmental storytelling to create a believable, oppressive Stone Age world.
Lighting: Dynamic lighting from torches and bonfires contrasts sharply with deep shadows. Crucial for mood and gameplay visibility.
UI Approach: Minimalist and diegetic where possible (e.g., heartbeat sounds/vignettes for health) to maximize immersion and minimize screen clutter.

Influences: *The Forest*, *Far Cry Primal*, *Hunt: Showdown*.

Sound Design: The Unseen Threat

[Placeholder: Abstract visual - sound waves / player reacting]
Creature Cues: Distinctive, directional sounds are vital. Hear the Wendigo's mimicry before you see it, the Werebear's heavy tread, the Werewolf pack's howls.
Ambient World: Crackling fires, wind, dripping water, distant unsettling calls create a living (and terrifying) soundscape. Dynamic music shifts with tension.
Player Feedback: Your own heavy breathing signals fear or low stamina. Footsteps change based on surface, warning nearby creatures (or players!).

Impact: Crucial for immersion, warning, jump scares, and locating threats.

Game Modes & Replayability

Click a mode for more detail.

Scavenge (1-4p)

The standard co-op experience. Work together to gather artifacts, secure bonfires, and escape. Sabotage is possible, adding tension.

Sabotage (2-4p)

PvPvE focus. Last survivor standing wins unique rewards. Expect betrayals as difficulty ramps up.

Solo (1p)

Face the horrors alone. Increased challenge with adjusted resource balance for a tense single-player experience.

Friendly (1-4p)

Pure co-op. Sabotage mechanics and friendly fire are disabled for a less stressful hunt.
