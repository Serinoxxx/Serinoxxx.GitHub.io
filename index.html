CAVEBREAKERS

A co-op survival horror game where Stone Age scavengers must outwit terrifying mythical creatures and each other to escape a cursed wilderness, balancing desperate teamwork with treacherous sabotage.

Face Your Fears (and Friends?)

Battle nightmarish creatures with unique AI... or sacrifice your buddy to the Wendigo. Hey, survival's tough! [cite: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

  • Wendigo: Mimicking voices, fears fire.
  • Werebear: Brute strength, attracted to scents.
  • Werewolf: Pack hunters, fast and agile.

Team Up or... Screw Up?

Will you be the hero or the hilarious villain? Sabotage is not just possible; it's sometimes *necessary*. (We're not responsible for broken friendships.) [cite: 16, 17, 18, 19, 20, 21]

Gameplay: It's a Scream

Scavenge, craft, survive, escape... and maybe backstab a little? We won't judge. Much.

  • Deep Perk System: Become the ultimate survivor... or saboteur. [cite: 39, 40, 41, 42]
  • Procedural Maps: Because dying in the same spot is boring. [cite: 37, 38]
  • Intense Atmosphere: Our sound design will make you check your closet. Seriously. [cite: 78, 79, 80]

The Loop: Die, Repeat, Get Revenge

Search, scavenge, craft, survive, escape, upgrade. The usual. Oh, and try not to get eaten. Again.
